Best of Bengaluru

Savour the best!

Bengaluru, the vibrant capital of Karnataka, offers a rich tapestry of culinary delights that reflect its diverse culture. From the iconic dosas and idlis of South Indian breakfast staples to the spicy, tangy flavors of Bisi Bele Bath, the city’s food scene is a blend of traditional and modern tastes. Street food lovers can indulge in crispy vada, pungent masala puri, and the ever-popular chaat. With an array of regional influences, Bengaluru’s food also embraces flavors from the North, from kebabs to parathas, making it a true food lover's paradise.

Hidden Gem of Bengaluru

Thom's Bakery

Thom's Bakery in Bengaluru is a beloved neighborhood bakery with a legacy of over 70 years. Located in the city’s bustling areas, particularly in the Fraser Town region, Thom’s Bakery is known for its traditional approach to baking and its wide variety of fresh, high-quality treats. Popular for its soft and fluffy bread, rich cakes, and delicious pastries, Thom's Bakery has become an iconic spot for locals who enjoy comforting, home-style baked goods.

Speciality of Bengaluru

Biriyani

For Bangaloreans, biryani isn’t just a dish—it’s an experience. The rich aroma, the perfect blend of spices, and the tender meat or veggies, all come together to create something truly special. Whether it's a lazy weekend treat, a family gathering, or a late-night craving, biryani is always the answer. From iconic spots in Shivajinagar to cozy local joints, Bangalore’s biryani is a love affair we can’t get enough of..
